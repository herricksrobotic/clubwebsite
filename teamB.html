Team B

Always inquisitive in nature, members of Team B decided to take on the “Electronics Online Challenge Sponsored by Texas Instruments” to dissect an electronic PH detector to figure out how it works. They learned about the basic principles of engineering and phyiscs. Team B also participated in the “‘Make It Real’ CAD Engineering Challenge,” where they designed a new and improved robotics part to improve its functionality or overcome an existing problem. Participating in the “‘Thinking Outside the Box’ Photography Challenge,” members of this team took unique and notable pictures relating to their interests in robotics.

Team Captain: Alexa LaPinta
Chief of Engineering: Dev Shah

Members: Selina and Ludia
