Team C

Channeling their creativity, team C participated in the “VRC Promote Award Online Video Challenge.” They made a video introducing our club to our local community to recruit new members and gain support for the team. Team C also participated in the “‘Make It Real’ CAD Engineering Challenge, designing a novel robotics part, and the “‘Thinking Outside the Box’ Photography Challenge,” where they drew on inspiration from robotics to take a picture meaningful to them.
